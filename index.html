Hybrid fuel cost: Full hybrids (e.g. Toyota Corolla Hybrid, Honda Fit Hybrid) achieve ~20–22km/L in Singapore conditions — about 45% better than a standard petrol car. This is estimated at 21km/L at $2.80/L. Mild hybrids get ~16–17km/L; this calculator uses full hybrid rates. Maintenance costs are similar to petrol — the self-charging battery rarely needs replacement.
EV fuel cost: Estimated at 6.5km/kWh at $0.40/kWh — a blended rate reflecting that most Singapore EV owners use a mix of home charging (~$0.30/kWh) and public AC charging (~$0.40–0.65/kWh). HDB residents without home chargers will be closer to the public rate. This is roughly 55–60% cheaper per km than petrol.
